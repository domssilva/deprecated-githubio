The Problem:

Old way of visualizing the bus stops in my city. I wanted to contribute to my comunity with a modern, easy and fast way of understanding the bus system of Verbania. Unfortunately the only available system we have is looking at papers and lots of PDFs. Whenere I wanted to pick a bus I always noted confused and puzzled people trying to understand the timestamps and which bus to pick; That's when I understood I could do something about that!

My Solution:

Unfortunately there is not programmatic interface available for me to fetch all the bus timestamps and lines (that would be too easy, wouldn't it?): The only resource I had were the PDFs!

Plan:

  1. scrape the data from the PDFs
  2. clean and model the data
  3. store it in a db
  4. serve data in a RESTful API
  5. build a dynamic interface for the client
